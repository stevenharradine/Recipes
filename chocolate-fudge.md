# Chocolate Fudge

## Ingredients
 * 1 cup sugar
 * ⅓ cup cocoa

### Stir in:
 * ¼ cup butter
 * ¼ cup milk
 * 1 tbsp corn syrup

### After heating
 * 2½ - 3 cups of icing sugar
 * 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions
1. Bring to boil for 1 minute stirring constantly.
2. Remove from heat and immediatly beat in icing sugar and vanilla
3. Turn into 8" x 8" pan and pat out, put in fridge.
