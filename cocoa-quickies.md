# Cocoa quickies

## Ingredients
 * ½ cup butter
 * 1 cup sugar
 * ½ cup cocoa
 * ½ cup milk
 * 1 tsp vanilla
 * 3 cups rolled oats
 * 1 cup coconut

## Directions
1. add butter, sugar, cocoa, milk and vanilla to pot and bring to a boil for 5 minutes
2. add rolled oats and coconut
3. drop on waked paper on cookie sheet and place in fridge
